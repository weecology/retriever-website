Available Datasets

The following datasets are currently available via the EcoData Retriever.

  • North American Breeding Bird Survey (both 10 and 50 stop)
  • Forest Inventory & Analysis
  • Alwyn Gentry’s Forest Transect Data
  • Species composition and abundance of mammalian communities
  • National Phenology Network
  • Plant Taxonomy – USDA
  • Life history characteristics of placental non-volant mammals
  • Phylogeny and metabolic rates in mammals
  • Predator and prey body sizes in marine food webs
  • Avian body sizes in relation to fecundity, mating system, display behavior, and resource sharing
  • Long-term monitoring and experimental manipulation of a Chihuahuan Desert ecosystem
  • Long-term mapped quadrats from Kansas prairie: demographic information for herbaceous plants
  • Tree growth, mortality, physical condition, and microsite in an old-growth lowland tropical rain forest
  • Thirty years of permanent vegetation plots, Mount St. Helens, Washington
  • A 12-year study on the scaling of vascular plant composition in an Oklahoma tallgrass prairie
  • Body mass of late Quaternary mammals
  • PanTHERIA: a species-level database of life history, ecology, and geography mammals
  • Densities and cover data for intertidal organisms in the Gulf of Maine, USA, from 2003 to 2007
  • Forest stand structure and composition along environmental gradients in the central Western Ghats of India
  • Multi-decade, spatially explicit population studies of canopy dynamics in Michigan old-growth forests
  • Mapped quadrats in sagebrush steppe: long-term data for analyzing demographic rates and interactions
  • Bioclim: climate data for ecological modeling and GIS
  • Mammal Supertree from Fritz et al. 2009
  • MammalDIET: comprehensive global dataset of diet preferences of mammals
